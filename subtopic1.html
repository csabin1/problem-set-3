National Meets and Cuts

In swimming, there are swim meets that are hosted by USA Swimming, which are known as National Meets. For these swim meets, you need to achieve certain times to qualify. The faster times you achieve, the higher the meet level is. Each meet has a certain number of stars, which is their rating. The ratings range from 1-5 stars, with 1 being the slowest ranked meet and 5 being the highest ranked meet. The order of the National Meets ranked from slowest to fastest is:

  1. Sectionals
  2. Futures Championships
  3. Junior National Championships
  4. Arena Pro Swim Series
